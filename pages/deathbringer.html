Deathbringer

Background

The still living body of Doombringer. Intent on rage rather than justice, its skull burned from his previous death by your hands.

Behavior

Deathbringer will punch you, sacrifice his own units to deal more damage to you, and go into a berserk rage each phase to rush your base and obliterate you. On top of this, he is much faster than his old form.
