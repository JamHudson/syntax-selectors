Deathbringer

Background

The charred, yet still living body of Doombringer. Intent on revenge rather than justice.

Behavior

Deathbringer will punch you, sacrifice his own units to deal more damage to you, and go into a berserk rage each phase to rush your base and obliterate you. On top of this, he is much faster than his old form.

In his first phase, he will simply walk towards your units and punch them, similar to his normal form final phase.

Upon activating his second phase, he will kill all of his allies. For each unit killed, he will gain more damage. Deathbringer's attacks also change to a three-hit combo, the last hit reaching further and dealing more damage.

Once reaching a very low amount of health, Deathbringer will be knocked away and over. After a short delay, he'll rise to his feet spouting rage induced gibberish. He'll then begin spinning and rapidly approaching your base. Each spin will deal damage in a large area to any unit in his range.

Upon being defeated, Deathbringer will let out an even more rage-filled scream, fully disintegrating in the process.

Tips

Deathbringer is an oppressive boss, especially early game. Deathbringer's absurd damage (especially in 3-star difficulty) invalidates the use of most meatshields aside from Retro/Atari, or cheap spam units like Battler.

The most important part of this level is stalling Deathbringer long enough to begin spamming Retro battler. The best way to do this is spawning normal Battlers while upgrading bank and gathering Tix Collectors.

The first issue you'll run into is the beginning wave of units. They are best dealt with by grouping them together and killing them all at once with the Slammer Cannon.
