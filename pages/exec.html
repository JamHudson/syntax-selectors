EXEC

Background

EXEC is the assumed executive of Innovation Inc laboratories. He is fought underground in Area 51.

Behavior

EXEC is a black-traited boss. He attacks at range and stuns on hit. After enough shots, he'll input a code onto his tablet before activating a random ability. The nature of the attack varies depending on the number of units on the field. When at an advantage, the abilities will be support-oriented. When at a disadvantage, the abilities will be offense-oriented.

In phase two, he'll input ":overclock" onto his tablet and teleport back to his base. He'll repeat the same behavior as phase one, albeit attacking faster meaning he uses his ability more.

In phase three, he'll input giberrish onto his tablet before teleporting back to his base again. This time, his base will be moved further away from yours (an ability unique to this boss) and every unit will glitch. EXEC will continue using abilities, typing gibberish onto his device, while also speeding up once again aswell as turning red.

Glitched units play random sounds, animations, and wear incorrect clothing. This is mostly a visual change as their behavior remains the same. Any unit spawned while EXEC is alive after this point will also be glitched.

Upon defeating EXEC, he'll begin glitching himself, teleporting randomly around the arena. This last a second before EXEC vanishes completely.
