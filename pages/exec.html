Exec

Background

Exec is the assumed executive of Innovation Inc laboratories. He is fought in Area 51.

Behavior

Exec is a black-traited boss. He attacks at range and stuns on hit. After enough shots, he'll input a code onto his tablet before activating a random ability. The nature of the attack varies depending on the number of units on the field. When at an advantage, the abilities will be support-oriented. When at a disadvantage, the abilities will be offense-oriented.
