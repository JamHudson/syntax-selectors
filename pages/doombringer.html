Doombringer

Background

Doombringer is the assumed guardian of the Doomspires. Considering his weapon, the "Banhammer", Doombringer is likely a force against the unruly and guilty. The implications of such an individual being the player's first opponent lends credence to the belief that the player is not morally correct.

Behavior

Doombringer is slow moving and needs range. He'll walk forward with his hammer and smash the ground when in range of a unit. After enough swings, he'll charge up his hammer before swinging a devastating blow, ignoring knockback immune units. After another set of hammer swings, he'll reach to the sky before equipping all of his current allies with bucket helmets, increasing their defense.
