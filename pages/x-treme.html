X-Treme

Background

X-Treme is a robot mech-suit piloted by Exec. The suit is sentient and prone to failure, as indicated by its evacuation feature not working.

Behavior

X-Treme shoots a plasma gun that blows up units as well as stuns them. It'll alternate between shooting grenades, obliterating the front line with a gatling gun, and lasering high-health units with its laser eyes.
